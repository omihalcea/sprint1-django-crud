Movie Management Project

An application developed with Python and Django to manage movies. It includes a complete authentication system with registration, login, logout, and CRUD features to organize your favorite movies.

Login

Access the platform to manage your movie collection. The login functionality ensures that only authorized users can enter.

User Registration

Sign up and create your own account. The system allows new users to easily join and manage their movie collection.

Logout

Securely end your session with the integrated logout system, ensuring the privacy of your account and personal data.

Movie Management (CRUD)

Add, view, edit, and delete movies from your collection. The CRUD system gives you full control over your movie library.
